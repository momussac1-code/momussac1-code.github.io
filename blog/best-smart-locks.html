5 Best Smart Locks for UK Homes (2026 Guide)

Published May 2026 — 8 min read

Smart locks are becoming increasingly popular in UK homes, offering keyless entry, remote access, and integration with smart home systems. Whether you're a homeowner looking to upgrade security or a landlord managing multiple properties, there's a smart lock for you.

This article contains affiliate links. We may earn a small commission if you purchase through these links, at no extra cost to you.

1. Yale Linus Smart Lock

The Yale Linus is the UK's most popular smart lock for good reason. It fits over your existing deadlock, works with most UK doors, and connects to your phone via Bluetooth and Wi-Fi. Key features include auto-lock, remote access via the Yale app, and compatibility with Amazon Alexa, Google Assistant, and Apple HomeKit.

Price: Around £179
Best for: Most UK homes

2. August Smart Lock Pro (3rd Gen)

The August Smart Lock Pro installs over your existing deadbolt and lets you lock and unlock from anywhere. It features DoorSense technology that tells you if your door is actually closed and locked. Works with Airbnb for easy guest access.

Price: Around £220
Best for: Landlords and Airbnb hosts

3. Nuki Smart Lock 3.0 Pro

Nuki is a favourite among UK smart home enthusiasts. The 3.0 Pro offers ultra-quiet operation, compatibility with most UK euro cylinder locks, and excellent app connectivity. Supports Matter protocol for broad smart home integration.

Price: Around €249 (available on Amazon UK)
Best for: Tech enthusiasts

4. Ultion Nuki Smart Lock

A British-made smart lock that combines military-grade security with smart features. The Ultion Nuki offers a unique "one-key" system where you can use your key, the app, or voice control. It's the only smart lock with a 3-year guarantee.

Price: Around £199
Best for: Security-conscious homeowners

5. Lockly Secure Pro

The Lockly Secure Pro features a 3D fingerprint sensor, a secure PIN entry system that changes the number layout each time (to prevent shoulder surfing), and works with your existing euro cylinder. It's one of the most secure smart locks on the market.

Price: Around £299
Best for: Maximum security

Which Smart Lock Should You Choose?

For most UK homes, we recommend the Yale Linus Smart Lock. It's affordable, easy to install, and works with most UK doors. If you're a landlord or manage rental properties, the August Smart Lock Pro's remote access and guest management features make it worth the extra cost.
